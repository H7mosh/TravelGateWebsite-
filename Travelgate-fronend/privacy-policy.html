Privacy Policy

Learn how we handle your personal data, and how we are committed to protecting it and providing a safe experience while using our services.

1

Introduction

Travelgate is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our website, mobile applications, or services. By using our services, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2

Information Collected

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal data provided during booking (name, passport number, contact information)
  • Payment and financial transaction information
  • Technical data (IP address, device type, browser, browsing history)
3

How Information is Used

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Processing bookings and providing services
  • Processing payments
  • Improving our services
  • Sending important updates and notifications
4

Information Protection

We store your data on secure servers and take measures to prevent unauthorized access. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes, except where necessary to complete your booking or as required by law.

5

User Rights

You have the right to:

  • View or modify your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements for financial records)
  • Unsubscribe from marketing messages
6

Information Sharing with Third Parties

We may share your information with:

  • Service providers (airlines, hotels) to confirm your bookings
  • We do not share your data for marketing purposes without your consent
7

Amendments to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to amend this Privacy Policy at any time. We will inform you of any changes by posting the updated policy on our website and, where appropriate, by email or other notification.

8

Applicable Laws

This policy is governed by the laws of the Republic of Iraq and local travel and tourism regulations. Any legal disputes shall be referred to the competent courts in Iraq.
